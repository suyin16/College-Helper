Factors to Look at When Applying to College

General Questions

Is the food good? How safe is the campus? How culturally diverse do you want the college or university to be? One way to answer these questions is by visiting the colleges that you are interested in because you get a sense of how the college is like culturally, socially, and academically.

An academic fit

Does the college have the majors that you want to study? Or if you don't know what exact career you would like to pursue, you should check if the college enables you to choose your major in your sophomore or junior year.

Activities
